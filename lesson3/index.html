Site Name

This website will be called "Southampton Chamber", with a URL of "sotonchamber.co.uk". It will serve as a hub for entrepreneurs to bolster the interests of their businesses and to attract more tourism to the city.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote local business interests, boost local tourism and connect the people involved in businesses with each other. The website will provide information about Southampton and its history, its siginificance in relation to commerce and its notable locations to visit. A list of local businesses will be available on the site, as well as the option to apply to become a member of the chamber, with details regarding all the benefits of membership. There will also be contact information on the site for all the officers and the board of directors.
