Wedding Receptions

At Temple Inn Hotel & Suites we offer services to help you host your wedding reception. Our reception hall can be customized to suit your wants and needs, and there is a kitchen available. We provide a paid catering service, or alternatively you may bring your own food and prepare it with the help of family and friends. Please contact us at inquirys@templeinn.com if you are interested in booking our reception hall, and we can make the necessary arrangements with you.
